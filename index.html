Hypothesis : What affects my energy levels the most?

To create this visualization I collected the following data:

  1. Amount of steps I walked in a day.
  2. Water Consumption in a day
  3. CalorieConsumption in a day
  4. Energy Levels (0 = Lazy and 5 = Energetic)

I collected the above data 4 times in a day - at 8 am, at 1 pm, at 6 pm and at 11 pm. 40 days of data has been adequate for me to come to a conclusion about how my Walking habits are affecting my Energy Levels.

Visualization

The X axis reprsents the Amount of Steps (in Thousands) and the Y axis represents the Energy Levels. The circles are plotted accordingly. The circle size represents the amount of water consumed in a day. The colors represent the amount of calories consumed in a day.

I have added a mouse over feature (tooltip). It displays the date, time, calorie amount, water level, Amount of steps, and Energy Levels for that observation. The slider at the top, makes this plot interactive. When the slider is moved, the circles animate from 8 am to 11pm. The color legend is Interactive as well. Once we click on a circle in the color legend, only the circle which has been clicked will be highlighted.

Conclusion

To Conclude, I now know the reasons for feeling less energetic on some days and more energetic on other days. Based on my visualization, we can see that Number of Steps I walk is having a major impact on my Energy Levels. Through this visualization, we can see that Calorie Consumption and Water Consumption do not seem to have as much impact as Amount of Steps do. The data that I have collected definitely proves my hypothesis, as I can see a pattern in my visualization and hypothesis.
